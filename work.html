A Complete Rebrand.

Captain James came to us with a 42-foot fishing charter and zero visual identity. We built everything from scratch — a custom name treatment, a hook-and-compass logo mark, and a full crew kit including performance shirts, caps, and deck bags.

The result? A 40% increase in bookings in the first season, driven largely by word-of-mouth and social media attention on their distinctive look.
