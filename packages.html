FAQ

Common questions.

What file formats will I receive?
+

You'll receive a complete file package including SVG, AI, EPS (vector), PNG, and PDF files — ready for vinyl cutting, embroidery, screen printing, or any digital application. We include both full-color and single-color versions.

Can I request changes after delivery?
+

Yes. All packages include revision rounds during the design process. After final delivery, First Mate clients get 1 year of minor change support, while Captain and Commodore clients receive lifetime support for adjustments to their brand files.

Do you print and ship the vinyl or apparel?
+

Our packages deliver design files only — we don't handle physical production. However, we work with recommended vendors and can connect you with trusted partners for vinyl cutting, embroidery, and apparel printing who already know our file specifications.

How does the design process work?
+

After submitting your order, you'll complete a brief creative questionnaire. Your dedicated designer will present 3 initial concepts, you'll select a direction and refine it through revision rounds until it's perfect. Then we package and deliver your final files.

Do you work on boats of all sizes?
+

Absolutely. We've branded everything from 14-foot bass boats to 120-foot superyachts. The package you choose isn't tied to your vessel size — it's about how much of the brand experience you want. Every boat deserves great design.

What if I don't have a boat name yet?
+

No problem at all. Many clients come to us without a name decided. As part of our brief process, we can help you brainstorm names based on your boat type, your personality, and the feeling you want to project on the water. Naming consultation is included in all packages.
