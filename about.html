Our Story

Born on the water,
built for the water.

Wake Design Co. was founded in 2017 by a pair of designers who were tired of seeing beautiful boats with generic, uninspired names slapped on their sterns with off-the-shelf decal fonts.

We believed — and still believe — that a vessel deserves the same creative care as any other brand. Whether it's a 14-foot aluminum fishing boat or a 100-foot superyacht, every boat is a statement about its owner.

Eight years later, we've branded over 340 vessels across 30+ states and 12 countries. Our studio remains small, intentional, and obsessively focused on maritime design.

How We Work

A process built for
serious craft.

We don't use templates. Every project starts with a blank page and a genuine curiosity about your vessel's story.

01

Discovery

We learn everything about you, your boat, and how you spend your time on the water. The brief isn't a form — it's a conversation.

02

Research

We study your vessel type, the maritime traditions connected to it, and the visual language that resonates with your lifestyle.

03

Design

Three unique concept directions, each telling a different story. We present them in context — on boats, on hats, on the water.

04

Delivery

A complete file package, placement guides, and ongoing support. Your brand is built to last as long as your boat does.

Our Philosophy

Design that lives
in the real world.

We never show a design on a screen. Every concept is mocked up on an actual hull, worn on actual hats, printed on actual shirts. Because your brand has to work on the water — not just in a presentation.

We test our work in sunlight, in salt air, and at speed. That's the Wake standard.

What We Stand For

Our values on the
and off the water.

01
